Nikola Tesla

1856 - 1943

Nikola Tesla was a brilliant inventor and electrical engineer known for his revolutionary contributions to the development of alternating curent electricity.

Key Achievements

  • Developed the Tesla coil, a high-frequency transformer still used in radio technology today.
  • Pioneered the development of AC (alternating current) electrical systems, which became the standard for power transmission worldwide.
  • Invented the induction motor, which is widely used in household appliances and industrial machinery.
  • Conducted groundbreaking research in wireless communication and energy transmission.
